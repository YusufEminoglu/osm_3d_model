# Showcase Playbook

Use this page to plan GitHub screenshots, demos, classroom walkthroughs, and release posts for 3D OSM Model.

The aim is simple: show that a real OSM area can become a useful, readable, attractive 3D city model in minutes.

## Core Demo Script

1. Open QGIS with 3D OSM Model enabled.
2. Zoom to a compact urban area or select a polygon.
3. Keep the maximum study area small enough for a fast Overpass response.
4. Click **Create OSM layers & export 3D viewer**.
5. In the viewer, open the dashboard and show live counts.
6. Toggle Layers, Style, Scene & Sun, and Walk mode.
7. Take a screenshot from a low golden-hour camera angle.

## Showcase Recipes

| Recipe | Best area | What to capture |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Compact neighborhood | Dense residential or mixed-use area | Function-colored buildings, floors, sidewalks, cars, pedestrians, dashboard metrics. |
| Complete street | Street with cycleway tags and street furniture | Bike lanes, cyclists, bus stops, benches, lamps, bins, traffic density. |
| Waterfront corridor | River, stream, canal, or drainage route | Water ribbons, tree belts, greens, sun angle, measurement. |
| Campus or civic core | Selected polygon around public buildings | Selected-area workflow, walk mode, camera bookmarks, clean screenshot. |
| Hillside district | Area with optional DEM available | Terrain base, DEM mesh quality, time-of-day shadows, topography view. |

## Screenshot Checklist

Capture these five images for an elite GitHub presentation:

| Shot | Camera | UI state |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hero city | Oblique aerial, golden hour | Dashboard visible, toolbar clean. |
| Street life | Low angle near a road | Cars, pedestrians, sidewalks, lamps visible. |
| Mobility | Aerial over a corridor | Bike lanes and cyclists enabled. |
| Landscape | Wide angle over greens/water | Waterways, trees, terrain base visible. |
| Style studio | Medium view of buildings | Style dock or function facade controls open. |
